Analysis of the lexicon used at the LA County Library

Lexicon Changes Suggested by Valerie Gross

In her 2013 book Transforming Our Image, Building Our Brand, author Valerie Gross makes a number of very specific recommendations geared towards shifting the lexicon of libraries away from traditional, and at times, dated and confusing terminology, towards a lexicon that will enable the public, and key stakeholders, to quickly see and realize the library’s valuable roll as an education center in their community. Gross asserts that this change in vocabulary will help to transform public perception of the library and transform that library’s image overall.

These suggestions will appear shocking at first to those of us who have been in the library world for a while, but are all designed to help libraries’ put their best foot forward.
