LA County Public Library Response to Covid-19

The library has created a dedicated webpage to ensure staff has adequate access to information and resources pertaining to Covid-19 and the ongoing global pandemic.

They have offered online access to:
  • Digital Library Cards
  • Job and Small Business Resources
  • Activities to do at Home
  • Food Resources
  • Story Times

Maker Spaces Repurposed

The library has come together to fill a much-needed role in producing personal protective equipment in their maker spaces. Librarians are problem solvers, makers, and care about their community.
